Small NFLX API

Instructions:
Get films by title:GET /movie/title/?s=train
Get first 100 films by release years between year1 qnd year2:GET /movie/year/year1/year2
Get films by category (children, family, adult):GET /rating/children
Get first 10 films by genre (sorted by release year desc):GET /genre/thriller
Get pairs (>2 times) to the two given actors:GET /2actors/?actor1=Rose McIver&actor2=Ben Lamb
Finds movies by type, year or genre:GET /movie/?year=2020&type=Movie&genre=Thrillers
